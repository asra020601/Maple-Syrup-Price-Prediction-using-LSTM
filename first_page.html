Maple Syrup Price Prediction using LSTM

Maple syrup is a natural sweetener derived from the sap of sugar maple trees. The price of maple syrup can be influenced by various factors such as weather conditions, production yield, and market demand. In this project, we aim to develop a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) model for predicting the price of maple syrup. By utilizing historical data on various relevant features, including production metrics, economic indicators, and environmental factors, we seek to create an accurate prediction model that can assist producers, traders, and consumers in making informed decisions.
